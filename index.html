Fairness verification

Every round is verified on Solana via Switchboard VRF. Enter a round number to independently verify the winner selection.
ROUND NUMBER
HOW IT WORKS
1
Commit
Before the round starts, Switchboard VRF commits a hidden random value on-chain. No one can see or change it.
2
Reveal
After the round ends, the VRF reveals the value. No one — including Savage — could predict it.
3
Draw
SHA256 entropy is derived from all inputs. The winning ticket = entropy mod total tickets. You can verify this yourself right here.
